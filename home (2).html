Where do I start ?

Well, if you are new here. Might as well your journey by REGISTER_ing first. After you have your own account You might want to check out for some Examinations available. Then, after choosing the one you are applying for, Click on it and you would be given an online form to fill. There you are sincerely expected to fill on the facts and your personal data. After you submit the form, you will be notified about the Acceptence via your E-mail. All other necessary details about when and where the exams are going to be held will also be provided along with some instructions.
