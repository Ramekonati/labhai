The story of a young man...

Long long ago, there lived a boy who had just completed his SEE. The board-exam was over and the vacation was on. So, like any other student he was completely wasting his time when he was expected to study for grade-11 or simply study brigde-course. Disobeying his parents, he didn't even study for the entire vacation. But as you can guess, it was just the silence before the strom because he had to apply for different collages for his upcomming studies. The boy was totally stubborn about not going anywhere he had to go out and fill up the forms himself. Insted of filling the forms, he just made excuses in front of his parents and did not apply in any collages. Those lies were later regretted by our founding member the boy himself (Mr. Ram). So, in-order to help other lazy ones like him, he came up with a great idea and established " appLIE ".



Where do I start ?

Well, if you are new here. Might as well start your journey by REGISTER_ing first. After you have your own account You might want to check out for some Examinations available. Then, after choosing the one you are applying for, Click on it and you would be given an online form to fill. There you are sincerely expected to fill on the facts and your personal data. After you submit the form, you will be notified about the Acceptence via your E-mail. All other necessary details about when and where the exams are going to be held will also be provided along with some instructions.
